About Barne's

We are a company which prides itself in providing reading resources to small communities. We are branched out in over 100 outlets, each one equipped with a library, a children reading area, a book store, 5 book club lounges and 2 platinum member lounges. We also have the largest collection of paperbacks, audio books and ebooks up for sale on our online platforms. Our stations are open every day and all are welcome. Read More
